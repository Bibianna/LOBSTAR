Some people think that getting advice form older people is more helpful than from peers. However, in my opinion, the advice from peer is better than the help from older people. There are two reasons. Firstly, people who are in same age with me can understand me better. Also, it is wrong think that older people might have more helpful advice. First of all, because your friends might also get the same problems that you have they can give you a help that you really need. For example, in my case, I had some problems with my study ways. It was so hard to find the best way to study science. In the past, I thought memorizing everything is good for studying science. However, I used to get terrible scores in my science tests. At that time, I have a friend who have similar score to mine. We thought about our way to study science together. Finally, we catch out our problems and study not by memorizing, but by understanding. Without my friend's help, I couldn't have got perfect score in my last exam of our high school.
